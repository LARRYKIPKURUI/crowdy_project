CrowdFund Projects Available

Estate Road Renovation
Target amount: Kshs 750,000

The Estate Road Renovation project seeks to improve the condition of roads within the estate, ensuring safer and smoother transportation for residents and visitors. The funds will cover resurfacing, pothole repairs, drainage improvement, and signage installation.

Why this project? Well-maintained roads are vital for enhancing mobility, reducing vehicle wear and tear, and improving overall safety. By contributing to this initiative, you are helping to create a better and more connected community.

Community Borehole
Target amount: Kshs 100,000

The Community Borehole project aims to provide sustainable access to clean water for underserved communities. Our goal is to raise Kshs 100,000 to cover drilling, installation, and maintenance costs. With your support, we can ensure a consistent water supply for the community, improving health and livelihoods.

Why this project? Lack of reliable access to water is a significant challenge in many areas. The community borehole will not only address immediate water needs but also contribute to long-term health and economic benefits. Your contributions will directly impact the lives of many.

Public Park Cleanup
Target amount: Kshs 75,000

The Public Park Cleanup project focuses on restoring and beautifying our local park to create a safe, clean, and welcoming environment for the community. The funds raised will cover the costs of cleaning supplies, waste disposal, landscaping, and maintenance.

Why this project? A clean park enhances community well-being, promotes environmental sustainability, and provides a space for recreation and social gatherings. Your support will directly contribute to improving the quality of life in our area.

Community College Construction
Target amount: Kshs 14,100,000

The Community College Construction project aims to establish a state-of-the-art educational facility that will provide access to affordable, quality education and vocational training for underserved communities. The funds will be used for construction, furnishing, and essential equipment to ensure students have a conducive learning environment.

Why this project? Education is the cornerstone of community development. By supporting this initiative, you are helping empower individuals with the skills and knowledge needed to build a better future for themselves and their families.
