Crowdfunding is a method of raising money from a large number of people, typically via the internet, to support a specific project, cause, or business venture.

Crowdfunding works by allowing individuals or organizations to raise money from a large group of people, often through online platforms. Through Crowdy, users can easily view various fundraisers that have accumulated contributions from supporters. In addition, Crowdy showcases available projects that fundraisers can contribute to, providing them with opportunities to support causes or initiatives they care about.
